Ordle

It's like Wordle, but it without the order!

How to Play

The game will give you a sequence of letters and numbers, the goal is to sort them out in increasing order from left to right. Arrange them so that each letter and their corresponding place in the English alphabet is in increasing order along with the regular numbers in the sequence.

How to Control

Use the A key on your keyboard to move your selection to the left, and use the D key to move to the right, the boxed letter with the white background indicates it is selected.

Use the Shift key on your keyboard to enable Edit mode, in this mode moving the selector using the A and D keys will bring the letter along with it.

To score a point, disable the edit mode once you're done and you will earn one point if it's correct.

The game's too easy, how do I turn it up a notch?

The game starts out with a twenty second time limit and as you play more, this time goes down to as low as 10 on the easiest difficulty. Additionally, the game will add the amount of numbers or letters in each sequence and goes up to 7 from the initial 5.

Still too easy?

You can also configure how difficult you want your game to be by editing the time limit and sequence size! Additionally bonuses are given to players who finish quickly. You can change the settings of your game, just below the play button.
