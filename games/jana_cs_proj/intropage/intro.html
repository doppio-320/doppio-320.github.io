Whack-a-Sus!

by Jana Maisei Venigas

Whack-a-Sus is an Among Us themed game with the mechanics that work like Whackamole!
The red impostor wants to get out but it is your job to protect the facility by preventing the impostor from going out of his hole!

There are five impostors and it is your duty to click and catch each one planning to escape before it is too late!

The game lasts for 3:40 seconds so sit tight!
Once the game has ended you can compete with your friends on who gets the best score!
